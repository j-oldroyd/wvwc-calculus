Theorem 11.3.13. Vector projection formulas.

Let \(\mathbf{a}\) and \(\mathbf{b}\) denote vectors in \(\RR^{2}\) or \(\RR^{3}\text{.}\) Then the projection of \(\mathbf{a}\) onto \(\mathbf{b}\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \proj{\mathbf{b}}{\mathbf{a}} = \frac{\mathbf{a}\cdot\mathbf{b}}{\mathbf{b}\cdot\mathbf{b}}\mathbf{b}\text{.} \end{equation*}
