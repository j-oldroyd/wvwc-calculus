Definition 14.2.6. Line integral of a vector field.

Let \(C\) denote a smooth curve and let \(\vb{F}\) be a vector field continuous on \(C\text{.}\) Then we define the line integral of \(\vb{F}\) over \(C\) to be \(\int_{C}\vb{F}\cdot\vb{T}\dd{s}\text{.}\)
