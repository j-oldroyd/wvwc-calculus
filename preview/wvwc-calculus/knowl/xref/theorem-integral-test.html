Theorem 9.3.3. Integral Test.

Suppose that \(f(x)\) is a positive, decreasing function on \([1,\infty)\) and let \(a_{n} = f(n)\text{.}\) Then
\begin{equation*} \int_{1}^{\infty}f(x)\,dx\quad\text{and}\quad \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}a_{n} \end{equation*}
must both converge or both diverge.
