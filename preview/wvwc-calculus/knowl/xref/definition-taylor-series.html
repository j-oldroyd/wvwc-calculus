Definition 9.7.7. Taylor Series.

Let \(f(x)\) be a function that is infinitely differentiable at \(x=a\text{.}\) The Taylor series of \(f(x)\) centered at \(x=a\) is
\begin{equation*} \sum_{k=0}^{\infty}\frac{f^{(k)}(a)}{k!}(x-a)^k\text{.} \end{equation*}
If \(a=0\text{,}\) we often call the resulting series the Maclaurin series of \(f(x)\text{.}\)
