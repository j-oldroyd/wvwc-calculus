Theorem 5.5.3. Mean Value Theorem.

Let \(f(x)\) be differentiable on some interval \([a,b]\text{.}\) Then there exists a number \(c\) in \((a,b)\) such that
\begin{equation*} f^{\prime}(c) = \frac{f(b) - f(a)}{b-a}, \end{equation*}
or equivalently
\begin{equation*} f(b) - f(a) = f^{\prime}(c)(b-a). \end{equation*}
