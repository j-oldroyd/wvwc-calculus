Definition 13.8.1. The Jacobian of a transformation.

The Jacobian determinant, or more simply the Jacobian, of the coordinate transformation \(x = g(u,v)\) and \(y = h(u,v)\) is defined by
\begin{equation*} J(u,v) = \pdv{(x,y)}{(u,v)} = \left|\begin{array}{cc} x_{u} \amp x_{v} \\ y_{u} \amp y_{v}\end{array}\right|\text{.} \end{equation*}
