Example 14.2.14. Verifying the flux.

Compute the flux integral in Example 14.2.13.
🔗
Solution.
We have
\begin{align*} \oint_{C}\vb{F}\cdot\vb{n}\dd{s} \amp = \int_{0}^{2\pi}P\,dy - Q\,dx\\ \amp = \int_{0}^{2}\brackets{-\sin t\cos t - \cos t(-\sin t)}\,dt\\ \amp = 0\text{.} \end{align*}
