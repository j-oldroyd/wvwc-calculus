Definition 14.2.12. Flux integral.

Let \(C\) denote a smooth curve and let \(\vb{F}\) be a vector field continuous on \(C\text{.}\) Then we define the flux integral of \(\vb{F}\) across \(C\) to be \(\int_{C}\vb{F}\cdot\vb{n}\dd{s}\text{,}\) where \(\vb{n}\) is the outward unit normal vector to \(C\text{.}\)
